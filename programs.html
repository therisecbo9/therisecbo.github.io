Our Programs

Empowering communities through education, skills, and mentorship

📚 Education Program

We provide a safe and supportive learning environment through our community library in Kahawa West. Children are guided in completing school assignments while we monitor their academic progress. We also offer sponsorship to children from financially disadvantaged families.

👩‍👧 Women Empowerment

We equip women and young mothers with practical skills such as beadwork, poultry farming, crocheting, and financial literacy. These skills enable them to generate income and support their families.

🧠 Mental Health & SRHR

We conduct mentorship camps during school holidays focusing on mental health awareness and sexual and reproductive health rights. We also provide sanitary support to underserved communities.

⚽ Sports & Talent Development

We support children in nurturing their talents and maintaining physical fitness through activities such as art, dance, chess, football, and Kho Kho. These programs promote confidence, teamwork, and discipline.

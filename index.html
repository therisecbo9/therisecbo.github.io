Who We Are

Founded in 2017, The Rise Community Based Organisation is dedicated to empowering women, youth, and children through education, mentorship, and skills development. We provide a safe space for learning, creativity, and personal growth within Kahawa West and beyond.

Our Programs

📚 Education

Community library, academic support, and sponsorship for children.

👩‍👧 Women Empowerment

Skills training in beadwork, poultry, crocheting, and financial literacy.

🧠 Mental Health & SRHR

Awareness programs, mentorship camps, and community outreach.

⚽ Sports & Talent

Art, dance, chess, football, and talent development activities.
